FULL DESCRIPTION
----------------
Venture CommunityPilot v1.2 is a 100% free, non-restrictive Discord bot built for multi-platform streamer communities. Automatically announce Twitch and Kick livestreams, YouTube livestreams and new video uploads, and send timed manual TikTok LIVE alerts. Server managers can customize channels and role pings per creator while also using Twitch Stream Teams, Live Roles, alert filters, role-based routes, private support tickets, and configurable stream-end actions. No feature is locked behind a premium tier; optional Ko-fi tips help cover hosting and continued development.
